Monkey D. Luffy is the fearless captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, destined to become the King of the Pirates after defeating Kaido and rising as one of the Four Emperors.

Roronoa Zoro is the swordmaster of the Straw Hat Pirates, renowned for his three-sword style and unwavering loyalty to Luffy on the path to becoming the world’s greatest swordsman.

Nami is the brilliant navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates, known for her weather mastery, cartographic genius, and fierce determination to map the entire world.

Usopp is the sharpshooter of the Straw Hat Pirates, a brave storyteller and sniper whose lies often become legendary truths on his journey to become a great warrior of the sea.

Sanji is the suave cook of the Straw Hat Pirates, a master of Black Leg martial arts and fiery kicks who fights to protect his crew and uphold his vow to never let anyone go hungry.

Tony Tony Chopper is the adorable doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates, a reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit and uses his transformations and medical genius to heal allies and fight with heart.

Nico Robin is the enigmatic archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates, a master of the Hana Hana no Mi and the only person alive who can read the ancient Poneglyphs that reveal the true history of the world.

Franky is the cyborg shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates, a cola-powered genius who built the Thousand Sunny and fights with explosive flair and radical style.

Brook is the skeletal musician of the Straw Hat Pirates, a soul-powered swordsman revived by the Yomi Yomi no Mi, known for his haunting melodies, comic charm, and undying loyalty to his crew.

Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, a noble fish-man and master of Fish-Man Karate who brings wisdom, strength, and unwavering honor to Luffy’s crew.
